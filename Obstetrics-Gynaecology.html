Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The Obstetrics and Gynaecology Department at Akshay Hospital provides expert care for women’s health, from routine check-ups to high-risk pregnancies. Our experienced team ensures personalized, safe, and compassionate care at every stage of life.

Comprehensive Women’s Health Care

Our Expertise

Our OB/GYN specialists provide care in areas like pregnancy, childbirth, reproductive health, and gynecological surgeries. We focus on personalized care, advanced treatments, and patient comfort.

  • Routine and high-risk pregnancy management
  • Infertility evaluation and treatment
  • Gynecological surgeries including minimally invasive procedures
  • Menopause and hormonal health care
  • Preventive screenings and health counseling

Why Choose Our OB/GYN Department?
